C3-PRO: Bridging the gap between Apple's ResearchKit and I2B2

What is C3-PRO?

C3-PRO is an ecosystem of FHIR DSTU-2 compliant software systems that enables the integration of Apple's ResearchKit data into i2b2 in a secured and highly scalable fashion.

i2b2 Integration

C3-PRO stores the gathered data into an i2b2 instance, the wide adopted scalable datamining system for clinical research.

Research Kit at a glance

C3-PRO uses Apple's ResearchKit framework to consent patients and to drive surveys and activity reports directly on patients' iPhones

FHIR DSTU-2 compliant

All C3-PRO subsystems use FHIR DSTU-2 standards. From the iPhone to i2b2, data is transmitted and captured by FHIR DSTU-2 compliant systems.

Highly scalable and secure

All data is sent encrypted from the iPhone, enqued in an Amazon SQS and unencrypted in-house using RSA and AES encryption algorithms.
